Lasagna. Tasty!

Lasagna: a multi-layered gift from the gods of taste and pleasure.

Ingredients:

  • Some layers of sfoglia
  • Tomato
  • Ragù
  • Besciamella

Preparation:

  1. Assemble the layers of sfoglia, tomato, ragù and besciamella like you have to build a five-story hotel.
  2. Observe your finest creation for a good 3 minutes, contemplating how such perfection can be achieved by the mortal being like you are
  3. Preheat the oven while you admire the dish
  4. Slam it into the man-enslaved hell and wait... i dunno... maybe half an hour?
  5. If it's hot and it looks ready, eat it. Achieve god-like status.
